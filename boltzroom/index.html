What You’ll Get When You Join

Here’s what happens when you step into the BoltzRoom circle:.

Real Conversations. Real Problems. Real People

Real founders share what’s working and what’s not. No filters, no fake success stories.

Real Accountability

We check in, not to judge, but to keep you moving — because business shouldn’t feel like survival every day.

Monthly “Fix My Business” Sessions

Live online meetups where experts and members troubleshoot one member’s challenge together.

Offline Founder Circles

Physical meetups in Lagos, Abuja, and Accra where we eat, laugh, and learn — not network in suits.

Learn Directly from the People Who Fix Businesses Daily

Priority invites to mini-clinics on sales, finance, automation, and customer experience..

Funding Readiness Support
